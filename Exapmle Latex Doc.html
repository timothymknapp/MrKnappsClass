MathJax Example

Here's an inline equation: \(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\)

And here's a block equation:

\[E = mc^2\]

And here's a more complex block equation:

\[ f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^\infty \hat f(\xi)\,e^{2 \pi i \xi x} \,d\xi \]

MathJax allows you to write LaTeX-style equations in your HTML documents. They'll be rendered as high-quality mathematical notation in any modern browser.
