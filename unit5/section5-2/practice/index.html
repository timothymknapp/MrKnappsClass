Ex1.

A conical tank with a radius of $ 4 \, \text{m} $ and a height of $ 10 \, \text{m} $ is being filled with water at a rate of $ 2 \, \text{m}^3/\text{min} $. How fast is the water level rising when the water is $ 5 \, \text{m} $ deep?

Solution
  1. $$ \frac{r}{h} = \frac{4}{10} = \frac{2}{5}. $$$$ r = \frac{2}{5}h. $$
  2. $$ V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h. $$$$ V = \frac{1}{3} \pi \left(\frac{2}{5}h\right)^2 h = \frac{1}{3} \pi \cdot \frac{4}{25} h^3 = \frac{4}{75} \pi h^3. $$
  3. $$ \frac{dV}{dt} = \frac{4}{75} \pi \cdot 3h^2 \frac{dh}{dt}. $$$$ \frac{dV}{dt} = \frac{4}{25} \pi h^2 \frac{dh}{dt}. $$
  4. $$ \frac{dh}{dt} = \frac{\frac{dV}{dt}}{\frac{4}{25} \pi h^2}. $$
  5. $$ \frac{dh}{dt} = \frac{2}{\frac{4}{25} \pi (5)^2} = \frac{2}{\frac{4}{25} \pi (25)} = \frac{2}{4 \pi} = \frac{1}{2 \pi} \, \text{m/min}. $$
$$ \boxed{\frac{1}{2 \pi} \, \text{m/min} \, \text{(or approximately } 0.159 \, \text{m/min)}}. $$

Ex2.

A spherical balloon is being inflated at a rate of $ 8 \, \text{cm}^3/\text{s} $. How fast is the radius increasing when the radius is $ 6 \, \text{cm} $?

Solution
  1. $$ V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3. $$
  2. $$ \frac{dV}{dt} = 4 \pi r^2 \frac{dr}{dt}. $$
  3. $$ \frac{dr}{dt} = \frac{\frac{dV}{dt}}{4 \pi r^2}. $$
  4. $$ \frac{dr}{dt} = \frac{8}{4 \pi (6)^2} = \frac{8}{4 \pi (36)} = \frac{8}{144 \pi} = \frac{1}{18 \pi} \, \text{cm/s}. $$
$$ \boxed{\frac{1}{18 \pi} \, \text{cm/s} \, \text{(or approximately } 0.0177 \, \text{cm/s)}}. $$

Ex3.

A ladder $ 13 \, \text{ft} $ long leans against a vertical wall. If the bottom of the ladder slides away from the wall at $ 2 \, \text{ft/s} $, how fast is the top of the ladder sliding down the wall when the bottom is $ 5 \, \text{ft} $ from the wall?

Solution
  1. $$ x^2 + y^2 = 13^2. $$
  2. $$ 2x \frac{dx}{dt} + 2y \frac{dy}{dt} = 0. $$$$ x \frac{dx}{dt} + y \frac{dy}{dt} = 0. $$
  3. $$ \frac{dy}{dt} = -\frac{x}{y} \frac{dx}{dt}. $$
  4. $$ x^2 + y^2 = 169 \quad \Rightarrow \quad 5^2 + y^2 = 169 \quad \Rightarrow \quad y^2 = 144 \quad \Rightarrow \quad y = 12. $$
  5. $$ \frac{dy}{dt} = -\frac{5}{12}(2) = -\frac{10}{12} = -\frac{5}{6} \, \text{ft/s}. $$
$$ \boxed{-\frac{5}{6} \, \text{ft/s}}. $$

Ex4.

A car is traveling west toward an intersection at $ 30 \, \text{mph} $, while another car is traveling north away from the intersection at $ 40 \, \text{mph} $. At a certain moment, the first car is $ 0.4 \, \text{mi} $ east of the intersection, and the second car is $ 0.3 \, \text{mi} $ north of the intersection. How fast is the distance between the two cars changing at that moment?

Solution
  1. $$ z^2 = x^2 + y^2. $$
  2. $$ 2z \frac{dz}{dt} = 2x \frac{dx}{dt} + 2y \frac{dy}{dt}. $$$$ z \frac{dz}{dt} = x \frac{dx}{dt} + y \frac{dy}{dt}. $$
  3. $$ z = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} = \sqrt{(0.4)^2 + (0.3)^2} = \sqrt{0.16 + 0.09} = \sqrt{0.25} = 0.5 \, \text{mi}. $$
  4. $$ 0.5 \frac{dz}{dt} = (0.4)(-30) + (0.3)(40). $$$$ 0.5 \frac{dz}{dt} = -12 + 12 = 0. $$
  5. $$ \frac{dz}{dt} = \frac{0}{0.5} = 0 \, \text{mph}. $$
$$ \boxed{0 \, \text{mph}}. $$
